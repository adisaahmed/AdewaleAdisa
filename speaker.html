Adewale Adisa – a consummate speaking professional!

Adewale Adisa is one of the youngest but certainly one of the most impressive public speakers on the international speaking circuit today. He has graced the global stage since 2002 and his humour, charisma and thought-provoking content and great delivery style have won over audiences around the world.

Adewale inspires organisations, teams and leaders to win by shifting the way they think, work and create value. As a trusted sage in the boardroom too, he inspires and initiates revolution, leading disruption for businesses to thrive Vusi has been a catalyst for change in businesses across the globe through his expertise in strategy, leadership and sales.

He lives out loud and challenges himself to accomplish at least one terrifying thing every year. Adewale’s credentials and experience speak for themselves, inspiring people from New York, Paris and London to Abu Dhabi, Rio de Janeiro and Dar es Salaam, to mention a few host cities.

In November 2015, Adewale was selected as one of the Top 12 speakers in the world by Meetingsnet Magazine, sharing the list with the likes of Arianna Huffington (of the Huffington Post) and James Curleigh (VP of Levi Strauss & Co.).

Adewale and his team elevate their clients’ thinking, the conversations they have with their suppliers and peers, the spaces they occupy, the status of their profession and the professional integrity of their craft.

As keynote speaker Adewale shares the fundamentals of building a great team and how to win in your market through:

– Disciplined execution.
– An environment of trust.
– Unflinching accountability.
– An obsessive customer culture.
